About Junior Talent Lab

The UBS Junior Talent Lab is a fresh learning format where mediamatics trainees receive real assignments from UBS-Professionals and implement them independently as a team. In a creative, self-organized environment, the learners develop practical solutions, strengthen their professional skills, and tackle new challenges.

The Lab offers a simple platform where professional employees can submit their project ideas and small tasks. The learners then review and work on these assignments, providing clear feedback upon completion. The system ensures structured workflows, open communication, and a transparent overview of all active projects.

This way, not only useful products and services are created, but also strong personalities for the future.

How to Use the Platform

Getting started is simple! Browse available tasks in the Requests section and click "Accept Task" on any project that interests you. Once accepted, the task moves to your Overview where you can track your progress and communicate with the task creator.

Requests: All new tasks from UBS professionals waiting to be accepted
Overview: Your active tasks that you're currently working on
Finished: Completed tasks where you can view feedback and results

When you complete a task, submit your work, and it will automatically move to the Finished section. The platform keeps everything organized so you can focus on what matters most – learning and creating great solutions!
