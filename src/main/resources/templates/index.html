Welcome to the UBS Junior Talent Lab

Our collaborative learning and project portal connecting UBS apprentices with professional employees. Explore real-world assignments, collaborate in teams, and develop valuable skills through hands-on projects while contributing to meaningful business solutions.

About Junior Talent Lab

The UBS Junior Talent Lab is an innovative learning platform that bridges the gap between education and professional practice. Here, media specialist apprentices receive authentic assignments from UBS professionals and work on them independently as teams, fostering creativity, self-organization, and practical problem-solving skills in a real business environment.

The Lab provides a streamlined platform where UBS employees can submit project ideas, tasks, and business challenges of various types - from creative photo assignments to technical development tasks. Apprentices can browse, accept, and work on these assignments, receiving guidance and feedback from experienced professionals throughout the process.

This collaborative approach ensures structured workflows, transparent communication, and clear project oversight. The platform tracks progress, facilitates feedback exchange, and maintains a comprehensive overview of all active and completed projects, creating a valuable learning ecosystem.

By working on real business challenges, apprentices not only develop technical skills but also gain insights into professional workflows, client requirements, and business priorities - preparing them for successful careers while delivering tangible value to the organization.

How to use the platform

Getting started is straightforward! First, select your role (Apprentice or Employee) from the dropdown menu in the header. Apprentices can browse and accept tasks from the Requests section, while employees can create new tasks for apprentices to work on.

For Apprentices:
Requests: Discover new tasks and projects submitted by UBS employees. Each task includes detailed requirements, deadlines, and specifications. You can filter by task type, department, or complexity level.
Overview: Your personal dashboard showing all active assignments you're currently working on. Track deadlines, communicate with task creators, upload progress updates, and manage your workload effectively.
Finished: Archive of completed tasks where you can review feedback, see final results, and reflect on your learning journey. This section also showcases your accomplishments and skill development.

For Employees:
Create various types of tasks including video, photo, slideshow, poster, flyer, poll and other assignments. Monitor progress of your submitted tasks, communicate with apprentices, and provide feedback on completed work.

The platform supports various task types including video, photo, slideshow, poster, flyer, poll and other assignments. Each task type has specific submission requirements and evaluation criteria. When you complete a task, mark it as finished through the platform, and it will automatically move to the Finished section for review.

Communication tools are built into each task, allowing direct messaging with task creators, progress updates, and collaborative feedback. The platform keeps everything organized and accessible, so you can focus on learning, creating innovative solutions, and building professional relationships within UBS.
