Welcome to the UBS Junior Talent Lab

A shared platform for learning and working together – connecting UBS apprentices with experienced professionals. Take on real business assignments, work in teams, and build valuable skills through practical, hands-on projects, while making meaningful business solutions.

About Junior Talent Lab

The UBS Junior Talent Lab is a modern platform that connects internal clients with real business practice. Mediamatic apprentices are assigned by UBS professionals and work on internal client requests independently in a team. This setup promotes creativity, self-organization, and practical problem-solving.

The Junior Talent Lab platform offers a user-friendly interface where UBS employees can submit a wide range of project ideas, from creative photo tasks to more technical development assignments. Apprentices can explore these opportunities, choose the ones that fit their education level and skills, and work on them while receiving support and feedback from experienced professionals.

This way of working together promotes clear workflows, open communication, and effective project management. The platform tracks progress, supports structured feedback, and provides a transparent view of all active and completed projects.

By working on real challengesprojects, apprentices build not only technical know-how but also a better understanding of how professional teams operate, what clients expect, and how business priorities are set. This practical experience prepares them for future career success while also contributing real value to UBS.

How to use the platform

Getting started is simple. First, choose your role (Apprentice or Employee) from the dropdown menu at the top of the page. Apprentices can browse and take on tasks through the Requests section, while employees can create new tasks for apprentices to complete.

For Apprentices:
Requests: Explore new assignments tasks and projects submitted by UBS employees. Each task includes clear requirements, deadlines, and specific details.
Overview: This is the dashboard showing all active assignmentstasks. Here, you can track deadlines, communicate interact with task creators, upload progress, and manage your workload efficiently.
Finished: A section where completed tasks are stored. Review feedback and reflect on what you’ve learned. It also highlights your achievements and the skills you’ve gained.

For UBS Employees:
You can create different types of tasks – including videos, photos, slideshows, posters, flyers, polls, and more. After posting a task, you can follow its progress, communicate directly with apprentices, and give feedback during or after completion.

The platform supports a wide variety of task formats, each with its own submission guidelines and review process. Once a task is completed, apprentices mark sets it as finished, and it automatically moves to the Finished section for review.

All tasks include built-in communication tools, making it easy to message, share updates, and exchange feedback. The platform keeps everything in one place, helping you stay organized, learn effectively, and build meaningful connections within UBS.
